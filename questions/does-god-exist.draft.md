- Creation Demands a Creator
    - The bible teaches that God bring everything into existence (Isaiah 42:5; Isaiah 40:28; Mark 13:19; Rev 4:11)

- Life Demands a Life-Giver (Genesis 2:7)

- (Scientific) Laws Demand a Law-Giver, otherwise everything will end up in chaos (Isaiah 33:22; Jam 4:12; Job 28:24-27; Hebrews 1:3)

- Design demands a Designer: "by **understanding** He established the heavens" Prov 3:19; Psalm 139:14; Genesis 1:26-28

- Fulfilled Prophetic Promises:
    - Predictions of Tyre and Sidon (Ezekiel 28:22-23)
    - Predictions of Jesus (Isaiah 7:14, Micah 5:2, Hos 11:1, Zec 9:9, Psalm 22:16,18; Psalm 16:10)

- Answered Prayers

> God also bearing witness both with signs and wonders, with various miracles, and gifts of the Holy Spirit, according to His own will? -- Hebrews 2:4 (NKJV)
