# The majority could be wrong

A lie stays a lie even if everyone beliefs it to be true.

> You must not follow **the crowd** in doing wrong. -- Exodus 23:2 (NLT)

> And the great dragon was thrown down, that ancient serpent, who is called the devil and Satan, the deceiver of **the whole world** — whe was thrown down to the earth, and his angels were thrown down with him. -- Revelation 12:9 (ESV)

# Church leaders could be wrong

Leaders could be deceiving or be deceived themselves even if they are leading the church.

> For when one says, “I follow Paul,” and another, “I follow Apollos,” are you not being merely human? What then is Apollos? What is Paul?
> 
> ...
> 
> So let no one boast in men. -- 1 Corinthians 3:4-5,21

> Do not put your trust in princes, nor in a son of man, in whom there is no help. -- Psalm 146:3 (NKJV)

I urge you to [study God's word](/bible) and ask God's Spirit to reveal the truth.
