- "the great dragon" = "the serpent of old" = Devil = Satan (Rev 12:9)

- Enemy of Israel (1 Chr 21:1)

- Fell from Heaven (Luke 10:18)

- What Satan can do:

    - Got access to the LORD to accuse/oppose people (Job 1:6-12;
      2:1-7; Zac 3:2-3)

        - Job (1:8-11; 2:1-7)

        - Joshua the high priest (Zac 3:2-3)

    - Does not know everything ("speaks from his own") (John 8:44)

        - Can search the earth (Job 1:7; Job 2:2)
    - Can steal, destroy, kill property - only if God allows (1 Corinthians
      5:4-5), e.g.

        - Job (1:11-12)
    - Can cause sickness and pain - only if God allows, e.g.

        - Job (2:7)

        - Woman with 18 years of infirmity (Luke 13:12)

    - Can speak through a believer or tempt a believer to take the
      wrong action (1 Corinthians 7:5), e.g.

        - David (1 Chr 21:1)

        - Peter (Matthew 16:22-23)

        - Jesus - failed attempt (Matthew 4:1-11)

        - Judas Iscariot (Luke 22:3; John 13:27)

        - Ananias (Acts 5:3)

    - Take advantage of people via unforgiveness (2 Corinthians 2:3-11; Ephesians
      4:26-27)

    - Can transform himself

        - into an angel of light (2 Corinthians 11:14)
    - Interfere with ministry (1 Thes 2:18)

    - Can remove/plant his own thoughts/desires in people's "hearts"
      (Luke 8:12)

        - Adam & Eve (Genesis 3)

        - Judas Iscariot (John 13:2)

    - Take people into bondage with fear (Acts 10:38; Hebrews 2:14-15)

    - Is a murderer (John 8:44)

    - Speaks lies (John 8:44)

- Can also be a "father" to people, so that people have the same
  desires as the devil (John 8:44)

    - Elyas the sorcerer (Acts 13:10)

## Who is Lucifer?

- A fallen angel, because he tried (Isaiah 14:12-21)

    - to exalt his throne above the starts of God

    - want to sit on the mount of the congregation on the farthest
      sides of the north

    - want to accent above the heights of the clouds

    - want to be like the Most High

- Weakens nations (Isaiah 14:16-17):

    - shook kingdoms

    - made the world a wilderness

    - destroyed cities

    - made prisoners

- Will go to Sheol (lowest depths of the Pit)
